Past Jobs

VSLA / Financial Literacy Capacity Building – WORLD Vision Kenya Bandaptai ADP Bomet Livelihood Resilience Program.

The main objective of the project was to improve the quality of financial inclusion program in World Vision Kenya Bandaptai program in Bomet County. This entailed organizing, together with civil society leaders, a series of information meetings/ training at county, sub county and ward levels on the importance of ensuring high quality of financial ...inclusion through village savings and loans methodology to the community, conducting spot-check supervisory visits to the saving groups so as to monitor the quality of groups activities in the preparation of micro plans and cascade training from umbrella groups to individual groups. Building the capacity of groups on VSLA methodology and training them on financial literacy / business skills provided avenue for improvement of livelihood amongst the beneficiaries.
